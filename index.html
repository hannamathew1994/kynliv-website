About Us

KYNLiV is at the forefront of immunotherapeutic drug discovery, targeting autoimmune diseases and cancers. We pioneer the development of innovative immunotherapies that address both autoimmunity and cancer, aiming to balance the immune system by reprogramming immune cells to selectively suppress harmful immune responses while preserving the body's natural defense mechanisms.

Our Mission

Our mission is to advance a pipeline of proprietary drug candidates toward clinical trials. We're committed to developing more precise and effective treatments, avoiding the general suppression of the immune system. Our approach targets organ-specific immune regulation to create therapies that could potentially transform treatment options for millions of patients.
